Our Purpose

Affordable, Effective Eyecare
for Everyone.

Founded in 1977 by the Siremull Hirachand Charitable Trust, this hospital was built on a single conviction: quality eye care should not be a privilege. Nearly 50 years on, that conviction drives every consultation, every surgery, every pair of glasses we provide — at cost, or free, to whoever needs it.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Eye Care

Comprehensive Eye Checkup

Computerised examination covering vision, intraocular pressure, and full eye health assessment.

Cataract Micro Surgery

Phaco emulsification with foldable IOL implantation. Day procedures, rapid recovery.

Glaucoma Surgery

Trabeculectomy and YAG laser iridectomy for effective pressure management.

Retina Speciality Clinic

Diagnosis and laser treatment for diabetic retinopathy, retinal detachment, and macular disease.

Refractive Surgery

Precise correction of short-sightedness, long-sightedness, and astigmatism.

Paediatric Ophthalmology

Squint correction, amblyopia treatment, and early vision screening for children.

YAG Laser Procedures

Capsulotomy for post-cataract clouding and iridectomy for angle-closure glaucoma.

OCT Imaging

Optical coherence tomography for detailed cross-section imaging of the retina.

Retinal Laser Treatment

Iridex green laser therapy for blinding retinal diseases including diabetic retinopathy.

Optical Biometry

Zeiss IOL Master 700 — precise lens power calculation before cataract surgery.

Visual Field Analysis

Octopus automated perimetry to map and track glaucoma progression over time.

Opticals & Contact Lens

Prescription glasses and contact lenses at affordable rates, fitted on-site.

Our Technology

Advanced Equipment
